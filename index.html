Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meat.

Preparation time
  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes

  • Preparation: 5 minutes

  • Cooking: 5 minutes
Ingredients
  • 2-3 large eggss

  • Salt to taste

  • Pepper to taste

  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil

  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meat,herb

Instructions
  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You van add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.

  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.

  4. Add fillings (Optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny at in trhe middle, sprinkle your choosen filling over half of the omelette.

  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minutes then side it onto a plate.

  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with additional pepper and salt if needed.
